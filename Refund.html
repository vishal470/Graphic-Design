Refund and Cancellation Policy

Refund and Cancellation Policy

Opportunities And You* is committed to providing our customers with the best possible service. However, we understand that sometimes things don't go according to plan. If you need to cancel your service, we want to make the process as easy as possible for you.

Cancellations

Cancellations can be made at any time by contacting us at support@oppotunitiesandyou.com. We will process your cancellation within 24 hours of receiving your request.

Refunds

Refunds will be issued within 10 business days of your cancellation request. The amount of your refund will be based on the amount of time that has elapsed since your initial purchase.

Refund Exceptions

The following services are non-refundable:

  • Services that have already been rendered
  • Services that have been purchased with a discount code
  • Services that have been purchased as part of a bundle

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our refund and cancellation policy, please contact us at support@oppotunitiesandyou.com
